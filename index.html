Queen

A platform for running scripts on many browsers.

"A platform for running scripts on many browsers?"

It's best to explain with an example.

Let's say you write down a number, and then tell a group of workers that the number you wrote down is less than a maximum number. The job of the workers is to yell out random numbers between 0 and the maximum number until you hear the right number.

Now lets say that the group of people are browsers. Browsers which are willing to do what you ask of them and have bidirectional communication with you while doing it. In this case, you are the Queen Server.

Here is how to make the above example a reality:

  1. Install Node.js
  2. Run "npm install -g queen" in your command terminal
  3. Run "queen http://queenjs.com/example.js"
